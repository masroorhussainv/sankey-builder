How to Use

Sankey Builder — Step by Step

1
Copy the extraction prompt

Click 📋 Prompt ▾ in the top bar. Choose CSV — With Prior Year if the annual report shows two years side by side (recommended), or CSV — Quick Extract for current year only.

2
Extract data from NotebookLM

Upload your annual report PDF to NotebookLM, paste the prompt, and it will return your flow data as CSV lines like:

Total Revenue, Net Sales, 25789, 22340
Net Sales, Cost of Sales, 11662, 10100
Net Sales, Gross Profit, 14127, 12240
3
Fill in details and paste data

Enter the Company Name, Fiscal Year, and Statements type in the left panel. Paste the CSV into the Flow Data box. Lines starting with # are ignored.

4
Generate

Click Generate → or press Ctrl+Enter. The chart renders immediately. A yellow warning bar appears if any flow balances look off — usually harmless when income streams converge.

5
Interact with the chart
  • Hover any band or node to see values and YoY % in a tooltip
  • Drag any node bar up or down to reposition it
  • Double-click a node bar to rename its label inline
  • ▲▼ YoY button toggles YoY delta labels (when prior year data is present)
6
Export

Click ⬇ Export ▾ to save as a PNG (high-res image with header and insight cards) or PDF (chart + full flow data table on page 2).

💡 Recent charts are saved automatically — find them in the Recent Charts section of the panel to reload without re-pasting data.
