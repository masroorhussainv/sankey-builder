? How to Use
1
Copy a prompt

Click 📋 Prompt ▾ → CSV — With Prior Year for comparative data (recommended), Quick Extract for current year only, or Multi-Chart Extraction to extract multiple charts at once.

2
Extract from NotebookLM

Upload your annual report to NotebookLM, paste the prompt, copy the output. Company name, period, currency and unit are extracted automatically.

3
Paste & Generate

Paste the output into the Flow Data box and click Generate → or Ctrl+Enter. For multiple charts, separate them with === CHART: Name === markers. Click any field in the chart header to edit it inline.

4
Work with multiple charts

Use tabs to switch between charts or click ⊞ Grid for side-by-side comparison. Click + Add Chart to create new empty charts. Drag tabs to reorder.

5
Interact

Drag any node or its label to reposition · Hover bands and nodes for detail tooltips · Double-click a node to rename it · ▲▼ YoY toggles change labels

6
Save & Share

🔗 Share copies a URL with the full chart including node positions · ⬇ Export saves as PNG, PDF, or JSON · Saved charts appear in the panel automatically.
